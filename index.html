About Us

SAE TKMCE stands to enhance the knowledge base of student members about recent trends in the engineering of mobility systems and to provide a forum for members to informally exchange views and ideas. We provide essential resources to help you stay at the forefront of the mobility industry. SAE Members access first-to-market technical insights, best-in-class technical resources, and connections with a global network of leading industry experts.
